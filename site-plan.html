Content

Home page

My name is Emma Davis. I am 23 years old and an aspiring artist. I have done artworks ranging from fanart of video games to art of my own created ideas and characters. Having been surrounded by amazing artists all my life such as my siblings and friends, I have had the great opportunity to learn from them. It was through the encouragement of these people that drove me to keep drawing and become the artist I am today. I post my works regularly on Instagram and sometimes on X (formally known as Twitter). I am forever grateful for the support I have received from friends and family. Almost all of my artworks these days are digital and I use the IbisPaintX program to do my work. Currently I have been interested in animation and have been testing the waters to see if it is something I would like to do in the future.

Commission Rates/Rules

I am open to accepting commissions so long as they fit in line with my skill set and commission rules! Rates: LINE/SKETCH: Half Body- $10-$15 Full Body- $20-$25 BASE COLOR: Half Body- $30-$35 Full Body- $40-$45 FULL RENDER: Half Body-$45-$50 Full Body- $50-$55 Message me through email or DM on social media for further questions and availability. References to what you have in mind would be preferred and help make the progress go much more quickly and smoothly. Payment will be required after an approved rough sketch then I will give updates as the piece progresses. When the piece is done, I will ask for permission to post the artwork onto social media sites. You are allowed to deny permission to the posting of your piece, remain anonymous on the post, or be tagged with your social media account on the post. Rules: I mainly have done work in Destiny 2 and Titanfall artworks but will be happy to accept any idea you may have! Animals are not my specialty but I am still willing to grow and do my best. I will not do art in the following fields: NSFW, Gore, Furry, LGTBQ Please understand that I am religious and will not do any work that goes against my faith or makes me uncomfortable. Should this cause you trouble I can refer you to other artists who would be happy to fulfill your request. I am sorry for any inconvenience this may cause. Follow me on Instagram or X(Twitter) for giveaways, discount events, or see what I am currently working on! Please understand that I may be more active on Instagram than Twitter.

My Art Progression

Every child tends to draw but I didn’t really start my journey until I was in the middle of high school. At that time I decided that if I wanted my ideas to come to life I had to do them myself. I have been drawing ever since. First it was only traditional art in cheap sketchbooks. Slowly over time my skill grew and I moved onto digital art, starting first with my phone screen and the tip of my finger. Finally, I had access to an Ipad and stencil. My artistic style grew greatly through the years and I am still learning more each time I pick up my stencil. I first opened my artstyle for commissions in 2023. Accepting projects from other people has allowed me to challenge myself and push my limits further. I still accept commissions today at prices that I feel match the value of my style and work. Check out my commissions page to learn more about my rates and rules!
